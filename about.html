About AU Compliance Index

AU Compliance Index is a free, independent technical specifications database for Australian mining and heavy equipment.

How We Source Data

All specifications are indexed from official manufacturer technical datasheets and publicly available product documentation. We reference Australian Standards (such as AS 1418, AS 2550, and AS 3533) where applicable to equipment categories.

Important Notice

This site is an independent data index. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any equipment manufacturer. All data is provided for reference and estimation purposes only. Always consult official manufacturer documentation and certified engineers before making operational decisions.
